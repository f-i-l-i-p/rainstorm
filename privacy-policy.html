Privacy Policy

At regnkollen.se, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is used by regnkollen.se.

What personal data we process

  • Your geographic location.
This information is used to show you the weather at your current location. We do not store this information. You approve yourself, via a dialog box that your browser displays, if you want to give regnkollen.se access your position.

Cookies

regnkollen.se uses 'cookies'. These cookies are only used to store visitors' preferences. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing the web page content based on visitors preferences.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser options. To know more detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers, it can be found at the browsers' respective websites.
